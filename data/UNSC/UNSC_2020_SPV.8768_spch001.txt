The President (spoke in Russian): The Security Council will now begin its consideration of the item on its agenda. Members of the Council have before them document S/2020/1011, which contains the text ofa draft resolution submitted by the United States of America. I wish to draw the attention of Council members to document S/2020/944, which contains the report of the Secretary-General on the United Nations Integrated Office in Haiti. The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution to the vote now. A vote was taken by show of hands. In favour: Belgium, Dominican Republic, Estonia, France, Germany, Indonesia, Niger, Saint Vincent and the Grenadines, South Africa, Tunisia, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States of America, Viet Nam Against: None Abstaining: China, Russian Federation
