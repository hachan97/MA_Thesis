The President: As this is the first meeting of the
Security Council for the month of July 2014, I should
like to take this opportunity to pay tribute, on behalf
of the Council, to His Excellency Mr. Vitaly Churkin,
Permanent Representative of the Russian Federation, for
his service as President ofthe Council during the month
of June. I am sure that I speak for all members of the
Council in expressing deep appreciation to Ambassador
Churkin and his team for the great diplomatic skill
with which they conducted the Council's business last
month.
