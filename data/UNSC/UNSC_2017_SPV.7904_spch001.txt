The President: On behalf of the members of the
Security Council, I would like to condemn in the
strongest terms the cowardly terrorist attack that took
place in London on 22 March and resulted in the death
or serious injury of many innocent people. I would also
like to express my sincere condolences to the families
of the victims.

I now invite Council members to rise to observe
a minute of silence in tribute to the memory of the
victims of yesterday's attack and all other victims of
terrorism throughout the world.

The members of the Security Council observed a
minute ofsilence.
