The President (spoke in Spanish): I thank the
Executive Secretary of the Economic Community of
West African States (ECOWAS) for the kind words he
addressed to me.

We have listened attentively to statements by the
Secretary-General, by the Ministers or Ministers of
State for Foreign Affairs of Cote d'Ivoire, Ghana,
Guinea and Nigeria and by the Executive Secretary of
ECOWAS.

In accordance with the understanding reached in
the Council's prior consultations, I should now like to
invite Council members to a private meeting to
continue our discussion of this subject.

The meeting rose at 4.25 pm.
