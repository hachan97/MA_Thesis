The President (spoke in Arabic): The Security
Council will now begin its consideration of the item on
its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them
documents S/20l2/928 and S/2012/929, which contain,
respectively, the texts of two draft resolutions.

It is my understanding that the Council is ready to
proceed to the vote on the draft resolution before it. I shall
now first put to the vote the draft resolution contained in
document S/20l2/929, which was submitted by France,
Portugal, Togo, the United Kingdom of Great Britain
and Northern Ireland and the United States of America.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
Azerbaijan, China, Colombia, France, Germany,
Guatemala, India, Morocco, Pakistan, Portugal,
Russian Federation, South Africa, Togo, United
Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
and United States of America

The President (spoke in Arabic): There were 15
votes in favour. The draft resolution has been adopted
unanimously as resolution 2082 (2012).

I shall now put to the vote the draft resolution
contained in document S/2012/928, which was
submitted by France, Germany, Portugal, Togo, the
United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
and the United States of America.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
Azerbaijan, China, Colombia, France, Germany,
Guatemala, India, Morocco, Pakistan, Portugal,
Russian Federation, South Africa, Togo, United
Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
and United States of America


The President (spoke in Arabic): There were 15
votes in favour. The draft resolution has been adopted
unanimously as resolution 2083 (2012).

The Security Council has thus concluded the
present stage of its consideration of the item on its
agenda. The Council will remain seized of the matter.

The meeting rose at 3.30pm.
