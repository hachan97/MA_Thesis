The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them document
S/2015/570, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by Angola, France, Jordan, Spain, the United
Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland and the
United States of America.

I would like to draw the attention of the members
of the Council to document S/2015/518, which contains
the seventh report of the Secretary-General pursuant to
paragraph 4 of Security Council resolution 2107 (2013),
as well as to document S/2015/530, which contains
the text of the fourth report of the Secretary-General
pursuant to paragraph 6 of resolution 2169 (2014).

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:

Russian Federation, Spain, United Kingdom of

votes in favour. The draft resolution has been adopted
unanimously as resolution 2233 (2015).
