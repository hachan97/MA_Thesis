The President (spoke in Russian): I shall first put
to the vote the draft resolution contained in document
S/2018/516, submitted by Kuwait.

A vote was taken by show ofhands.

In favour:
Bolivia (Plurinational State of), China, Cote
d'Ivoire, Equatorial Guinea, France, Kazakhstan,
Kuwait, Peru, Russian Federation, Sweden
Against:
United States of America

Abstaining:
Ethiopia, Netherlands, Poland, United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland
The President (spoke in Russian): The draft
resolution received 10 votes in favour, one against and
four abstentions. The draft resolution has not been
adopted, owing to the negative vote of a permanent
member of the Council.

I shall now give the floor to those members of
the Council who wish to make statements following
the voting.
