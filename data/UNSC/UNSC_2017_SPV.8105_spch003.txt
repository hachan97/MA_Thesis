The President: The Council has taken note of
the Bolivian delegation's support for draft resolution
S/2017/933.

In accordance with rule 37 of the Council's
provisional rules of procedure, I invite the representative
of the Syrian Arab Republic to participate in
this meeting.

The Security Council will now begin its consideration
of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them
documents S/2017/933 and S/2017/962, which contain
the texts of two draft resolutions, respectively.

I now give the floor to those members ofthe Council
who wish to make statements before the voting.
