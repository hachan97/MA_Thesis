I have the honour to enclose herewith a copy of the briefing provided by Ambassador Marc Pecsteen de Buytswerve, Permanent Representative of Belgium, in his capacity as Chair of the Security Council Committee pursuant to resolution 751 (1992), concerning Somalia, in connection with the video-teleconference on the situation in Somalia, convened on Tuesday, 9 June 2020. A statement was also delivered by the representative of Somalia. In accordance with the procedure set out in the letter dated 7 May 2020 from the President of the Security Council addressed to the Permanent Representatives of the members of the Security Council (S/2020/372), which was agreed in the light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the coronavirus disease pandemic, these statements will be issued as an official document of the Security Council.
