The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

At the outset, Ambassador Roman OyarzunMarchesi
will make a joint statement on behalf of the Committee
established pursuant to resolutions 1267 (1999) and
1989 (2011), concerning Al-Qaida and associated

resolution 1540 (2004). Following that joint statement,

three Committees.
