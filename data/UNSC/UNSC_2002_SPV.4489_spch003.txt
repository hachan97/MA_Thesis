The President: I shall now put the draft
resolution to the vote, as orally revised.

A vote was taken by show ofhands.

In favour:
Bulgaria, Cameroon, China, Colombia, France,
Guinea, Ireland, Mauritius, Mexico, Norway,
Russian Federation, Singapore, United Kingdom
of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United
States of America

Against:
None

Abstentions:
Syrian Arab Republic

The President: The draft resolution received 14
votes in favour, none against and 1 abstention. The
draft resolution, as orally revised, has been adopted as
resolution 1397 (2002).

The Security Council has thus concluded the
present stage of its consideration of the item on its
agenda.

The meeting rose at 12 midnight.
