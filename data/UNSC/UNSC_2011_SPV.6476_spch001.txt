The President: As this is the first public meeting
of the Council for the month of February, I should like
to take this opportunity to pay tribute, on behalf of the
Council, to His Excellency Mr. Ivan Barbalic',
Permanent Representative of Bosnia and Herzegovina,
for his service as President of the Security Council for
the month of January 2011. I am sure I speak for all
members of the Council in expressing deep
appreciation to Ambassador Barbalic' and his
delegation for the great diplomatic skill with which
they conducted the Council's business last month.

Adoption of the agenda
