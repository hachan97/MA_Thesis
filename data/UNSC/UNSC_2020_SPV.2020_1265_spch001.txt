Ihave the honour to refer to Security Council resolution 2556 (2020), related to agenda item "The situation concerning the Democratic Republic of the Congo" and adopted on 18 December 2020. Resolution 2556 (2020) was adopted in accordance with the voting procedure set out in the letter by the President of the Security Council dated 27 March 2020 (S/2020/253), a procedure agreed in the light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the coronavirus disease pandemic. Pursuant to that procedure, I enclose herewith copies of the related documents: My letter dated 17 December 2020, addressed to the Permanent Representatives of Security Council members (see annex I), putting to the vote the draft resolution contained in document S/2020/1241 (see enclosure to annex J); Letters received in reply from Security Council members indicating their national position on the draft resolution (see annexes II to XVI); Statements subsequently submitted by Security Council members providing an explanation of their votes (see annexes XVII to XVIII); A statement subsequently submitted by the Democratic Republic of the Congo (see annex XIX). This letter and its enclosures will be issued as a document of the Security Council.
