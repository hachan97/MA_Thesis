I have the honour to refer to Security Council resolution 2531 (2020), related to the agenda item "The situation in Mali" and adopted on 29 June 2020. Resolution 2531 (2020) was adopted in accordance with the voting procedure set out in the letter by the President of the Security Council dated 27 March 2020 (S/2020/253), a procedure agreed in light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the coronavirus disease pandemic. Pursuant to that procedure, I enclose herewith copies of the related documents: e My letter dated 26 June 2020, addressed to the Permanent Representatives of the members of the Security Council (see annex I), putting to the vote the draft resolution contained in document S/2020/600 (see enclosure to annex I); ¢ Letters received in reply from members of the Security Council, indicating their national positions on the draft resolution (see annexes II to XVI); and ¢<< Statements received subsequently from Security Council members, providing explanations of their votes (see annexes XVII to XIX). The present letter and its annexes will be issued as a document of the Security Council.
