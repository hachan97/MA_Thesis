The President: We begin this meeting on
Afghanistan with a tragic reminder of the ultimate
price that too many have paid to help build the future
of their country. I would like to ask that the Council
observe a minute of silence in memory of all recent
victims of terrorism in Afghanistan, including the
victims of the attack on Kabul military hospital on
Wednesday, which claimed more than 50 lives and left
many more wounded.

The members of the Security Council observed a
minute ofsilence.

Adoption of the agenda
The agenda was adopted.
