The President (interpretation from Spanish): I shall
now put draft resolution S/ 1995/85 to the vote.

A vote was taken by show of hands.

In favour:
Argentina, Botswana, Czech Republic, France,
Germany, Honduras, Indonesia, Italy, Nigeria, Oman,
Russian Federation, Rwanda, United Kingdom of

America

Against:
None

Abstaining:
China

The President (interpretation from Spanish): The
result of the voting is as follows: 14 votes in favour, none
against and 1 abstention.

975 (1995).

I shall now call upon members of the Council who
