The President: Under rule 37 of the Council's
provisional rules of procedure, I should like to invite
the representatives of Timor-Leste, Japan, New
Zealand, the Philippines and Australia to participate in
this meeting.

to welcome His Excellency Mr. Kay Rala Xanana
Gusmao, Prime Minister and Minister of Defence and
Security of Timor-Leste.

Under rule 39 of the Council's provisional rules
of procedure, I shall take it that the Council decides to
invite Ms. Ameerah Haq, Special Representative of the
Secretary-General and head of the United Nations
Integrated Mission in Timor-Leste.

It is so decided.

Under rule 39 of the Council's provisional rules
of procedure, I invite His Excellency Mr. Pedro
Serrano, Acting Head of the delegation of the European
Union to the United Nations.

consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them
document S/2011/32, which contains the report of the
Secretary-General on the United Nations Integrated
Mission in Timor-Leste.
