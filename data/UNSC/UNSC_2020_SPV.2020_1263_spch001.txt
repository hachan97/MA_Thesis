I have the honour to refer to Security Council resolution 2555 (2020), related to the agenda item entitled "The situation in the Middle East" and adopted on 18 December 2020. Resolution 2555 (2020) was adopted in accordance with the voting procedure set out in the letter by the President of the Security Council dated 27 March 2020 (S/2020/253), a procedure agreed in the light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the coronavirus disease pandemic. Pursuant to that procedure, I enclose herewith copies of the related documents: My letter dated 17 December 2020, addressed to the Permanent Representatives of Security Council members (see annex I), putting to the vote the draft resolution contained in document S/2020/1232 (see enclosure to annex J); Letters received in reply from Security Council members indicating their national positions on the draft resolution (see annexes II to XVI). This letter and its annexes will be issued as a document of the Security Council.
