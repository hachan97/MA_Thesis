I have the honour to refer to Security Council resolution 2540 (2020), related to the agenda item "The situation in Somalia" and adopted on 28 August 2020. Resolution 2540 (2020) was adopted in accordance with the voting procedure set out in the letter by the President of the Security Council dated 27 March 2020 (S/2020/253), a procedure agreed in the light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. Pursuant to that procedure, I enclose herewith copies of the related documents: My letter dated 27 August 2020, addressed to the Permanent Representatives of members of the Security Council (annex I), putting to the vote the draft resolution contained in document S/2020/846 (enclosure to annex I); Letters received in reply from the members of the Security Council indicating their national positions on the draft resolution (annexes II to XVI); A statement subsequently submitted by a member of the Security Council providing an explanation of its vote (annex XVII); A statement subsequently submitted by Somalia (annex XVIII). This letter and its annexes will be issued as a document of the Security Council.
