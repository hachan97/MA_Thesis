The President (interpretation from Spanish): Before
proceeding to the vote, I would like to announce that
France has joined the list of sponsors of the draft
resolution.

I now put to the vote the draft resolution contained
in document S/ 1999/27.

A vote was taken by show of hands.

In favour:
Argentina, Bahrain, Brazil, Canada, China, France,
Gabon, Gambia, Malaysia, Namibia, Netherlands,
Russian Federation, Slovenia, United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States of
America

The President (interpretation from Spanish): There
were 15 votes in favour. The draft resolution has been
adopted unanimously as resolution 1221 (1999).

There are no further speakers on my list. The
Security Council has thus concluded the present stage of

its consideration of the item on the agenda.

The Security Council will remain seized of the
matter.

The meeting rose at 1.05 pm.
