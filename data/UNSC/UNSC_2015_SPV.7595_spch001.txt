The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

S/2015/1001, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by Angola, Chile, France, Jordan, Lithuania,
Malaysia, New Zealand, Nigeria, Spain, the United
Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland and the
