The President: In accordance with rule 37 of the

representative of the Republic of Korea to participate
in this meeting.

consideration of the item on its agenda.

S/2017/1092, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by the United States of America.

draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution

In favour:
