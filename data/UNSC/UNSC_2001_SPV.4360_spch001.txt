The President (spoke in Spanish): The Security
Council will now continue its consideration of the item
on its agenda. The Council is meeting in accordance
with the understanding reached in its prior
consultations.

Members of the Council have before them
document S/2001/828, which contains the text of a
draft resolution prepared in the course of the Council's
prior consultations.

It is my understanding that the Council is ready to
proceed to the vote on the draft resolution before it. IfI
hear no objection, I shall now put the draft resolution
to the vote.

There being no objection, it is so decided.
A vote was taken by show ofhands.

The President (spoke in Spanish): There were 15
votes in favour. The draft resolution has been adopted
unanimously as resolution 1366 (2001).

The Security Council has thus concluded the
present stage of its consideration of the item on its
agenda.
