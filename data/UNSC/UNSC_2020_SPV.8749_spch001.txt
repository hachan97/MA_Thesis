In accordance with rule 39 of the Council's provisional rules of procedure, I invite the following briefers to participate in the meeting: Mr. Carlos Ruiz Massieu, Special Representative of the Secretary- General and Head of the United Nations Verification Mission in Colombia, and Ms. Clemencia Carabali Rodallega, Member of the Municipal Association of Women and defender of Afro-Colombian territorial and human rights.
