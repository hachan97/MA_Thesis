The President: Members of the Council have
before them document S/2019/441, which contains the
text of a draft resolution submitted by the United States
of America.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it.

I shall put the draft resolution to the vote now.
A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
Belgium, Dominican Republic, France, Germany,
Indonesia, Kuwait, Peru, Poland, United Kingdom
of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United
States of America

Against:
None

Abstaining:
China, Cote d'lvoire, Equatorial Guinea, Russian
Federation, South Africa

The President: The draft resolution received
10 votes in favour, with five abstentions. The draft
resolution has been adopted as resolution 2471 (2019).

I shall now give the floor to those members of
the Council who wish to make statements following
the voting.
