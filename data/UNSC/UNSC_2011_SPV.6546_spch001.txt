The President (spoke in French): The Security
Council will now begin its consideration of the item on
its agenda.

At this meeting, the Council will hear briefings
by the five Permanent Representatives who led the
Security Council mission to Africa: first, His
Excellency Mr. Ge'rard Araud, Permanent
Representative of France and leader of the Addis
Ababa segment; secondly, Her Excellency Ms. Susan
Rice, Permanent Representative of the United States,
and His Excellency Mr. Vitaly Churkin, Permanent
Representative of the Russian Federation, who together
led the Khartoum and Juba segment; and thirdly, His
Excellency Sir Mark Lyall Grant, Permanent
Representative of the United Kingdom, and His
Excellency Mr. Baso Sangqu, Permanent
Representative of South Africa, who together led the
Nairobi segment ofthe mission.

I would like here to welcome the return of the
members of the Council and the Secretariat who took
part in the Council mission to Africa.
