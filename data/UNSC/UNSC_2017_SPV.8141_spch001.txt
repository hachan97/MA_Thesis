The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them
document S/2017/1063, which contains the text of a
draft resolution sumitted by Egypt, Japan and Sweden.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
Egypt, Ethiopia, France, Italy, Japan, Kazakhstan,
Senegal, Sweden, Ukraine, United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States
of America and Uruguay

Against:
None

Abstaining:
Bolivia (Plurinational State of), China and
Russian Federation,

The President: The draft resolution received
12 votes in favour, none against and 3 abstentions.
The draft resolution has been adopted as
resolution 2393 (2017).

I shall now give the floor to those members of the
Council who wish to make statements after the voting.
