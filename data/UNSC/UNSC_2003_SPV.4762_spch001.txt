The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda. The Security
Council is meeting in accordance with the
understanding reached in its prior consultations.

At this meeting, the Security Council will hear
briefings by Mr. Mark Malloch Brown, Mr. James
Morris, Mr. David Nabarro, Mr. Nils Kastberg and
Mr. Jakob Kellenberger.

At the end of those briefings, I will give the floor
to Council members who wish to make comments or
ask questions.

I wish to inform Council members that, due to the
late start of this meeting, it is my intention to adjourn
at 1 pm. and to reconvene at 3 pm. to continue our
consideration of the item. I regret any inconvenience
this may cause to any of our participants, including our
distinguished guests.

I welcome the presence of the Deputy Secretary-
General, Ms. Louise Frechette, and I invite her to take
the floor.
