participate in this meeting.

consideration of the item on its agenda.

S/2018/95, which contains the text of a draft resolution
submitted by the United States of America.

S/2017/1125, which contains the text of a letter dated

Sudan established pursuant to resolution 1591 (2005)
addressed to the President of the Security Council

draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
