The President (interpretation front Russian): The
Security Council will now begin its consideration of the
item on the agenda. The Security Council is meeting in
accordance with the understanding reached in its prior
consultations.

Members of the Council have before them the report
of the Secretary-General on the United Nations operation
in Cyprus, document 5/1995/1020 and Add. 1. Members of
the Council also have before them document 8/1995/1045,
which contains the text of a draft resolution prepared in the
course of the Council's prior consultations,
it is my understanding that the Security Council is
ready to proceed to the vote on the draft resolution
(5/1995/1045) before it. Unless I hear any objection, I
shall put the draft resolution to the vote now.

There being no objection, it is so decided.
A vote was taken by show of hands.

In favour.-
Argentina, Botswana, China, Czech Republic,
France. Germany, Honduras, Indonesia, ltaly.
Nigeria, Oman, Russian Federation, Rwanda,
United Kingdom" of Great Britain and Northern
Ireland, United States of America

The President (interpretation front Russian): There
were 15 votes in favour. The draft resolution has been
adopted unanimously as resolution 1032 (1995).

The Security Council has thus concluded the present
stage of its consideration of the item on the agenda. The

Security Council will remain seized of the matter.

The meeting rose at 6 p.m.
