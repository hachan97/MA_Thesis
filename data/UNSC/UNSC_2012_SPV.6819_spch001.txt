The President (spoke in Spanish): In accordance

procedure, I invite the representative of the Sudan to
participate in this meeting.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda.

S/2012/582, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by France, Germany, the United Kingdom
of Great Britain and Northern Ireland and the United
States of America.

to document 8/2012/548, which contains the report of
the Secretary-General on the African Union-United

proceed to the vote on the draft resolution before it.
