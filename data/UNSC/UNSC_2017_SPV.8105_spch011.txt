The President: I shall now put to the vote the draft
resolution contained in document S/2017/ 962, submitted
by France, Italy, Japan, Sweden, Ukraine, the United
Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland and the
United States of America.

A vote was taken by show ofhana's.

In favour:
Ethiopia, France, Italy, Japan, Kazakhstan,
Senegal, Sweden, Ukraine, United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States
of America, Uruguay

Against:
Bolivia (Plurinational State of), Russian Federation
Abstaining:
China, Egypt

The President: There were 11 votes in favour,
2 against and 2 abstentions. The draft resolution has not
been adopted, owing to the negative vote of a permanent
member of the Council.

I now give the floor to those members of the
Council who wish to make statements after the voting.
