The President (spoke in French): Under rule 37
of the Council's provisional rules of procedure, I invite
the representatives of Cuba, Costa Rica, Indonesia, the
Islamic Republic of Iran, Israel, Japan, Morocco,
Pakistan, Spain, Switzerland, the Syrian Arab
Republic, Turkey and the Bolivarian Republic of
Venezuela to participate in this meeting.

Under rule 39 of the Council's provisional rules
of procedure, I invite Mr. Pedro Serrano, Acting Head
of the Delegation of the European Union to the United
Nations, to participate in this meeting.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda.

At the outset, Ambassador Hardeep Singh Puri
will make a joint statement on behalf of the
Committees established pursuant to resolutions 1267
(1999), 1373 (2001) and 1540 (2004). Following this
joint statement, the Council will hear briefings by the
Chairs of those three Committees.

I now give the floor to Ambassador Puri.
