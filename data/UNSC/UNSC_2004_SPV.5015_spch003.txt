The President: It is my intention to put the draft
resolution to the vote now.

A vote was taken by show ofhands.

In favour:
Algeria, Angola, Benin, Brazil, Chile, France,
Germany, Philippines, Romania, Russian

America

Against:

None
Abstaining:

China, Pakistan
The President: The result of the voting is as
follows: 13 votes in favour, none against and 2
abstentions. The draft resolution has been adopted as
resolution 1556 (2004).

I shall now give the floor to those members of the
Council who wish to make statements following the
