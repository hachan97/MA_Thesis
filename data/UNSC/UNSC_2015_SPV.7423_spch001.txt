The President (spoke in Arabic): As this is the first
meeting of the Council for the month of April 2015, I
should like to take this opportunity to pay tribute, on
behalf of the Council, to His Excellency Mr. Francois
Delattre, Permanent Representative of France, for his
effective leadership of the Council as its President for
the month of March. I am sure I speak for all members
of the Council in expressing deep appreciation to
Ambassador Delattre and his delegation for the
great diplomatic skill with which they conducted the
Council's business last month.

Adoption of the agenda
The agenda was adopted.

Members of the Council have before them

Malaysia, Spain, the United Kingdom of Great Britain
and Northern Ireland, the United States of America and
the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela.

The Council is ready to proceed to a vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.
