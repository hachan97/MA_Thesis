The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them
document S/2017/1076, which contains the text of a
draft resolution submitted by France, Italy, Japan,
Senegal, Sweden, Ukraine, the United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland, the United States
of America and Uruguay.

It is my understanding that the Council is ready to
proceed to the vote of the draft resolution before it. I
shall put the draft resolution to the vote now.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
Bolivia, China, Egypt, Ethiopia, France, Italy, Japan,
Kazakhstan, Russian Federation, Senegal, Sweden,
Ukraine, United Kingdom of Great Britain and
Northern Ireland, United States of America, Uruguay

The President: The draft resolution received
15 votes in favour. The draft resolution has been
adopted unanimously as resolution 2395 (2017).

I shall now give the floor to those members of
the Council who wish to make statements following
the voting.
