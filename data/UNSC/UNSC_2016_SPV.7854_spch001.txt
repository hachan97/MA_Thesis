The President (spoke in Spanish): The Security
Council will now begin its consideration of the item on
its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them document
S/2016/1118, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by the United States of America.

I wish to draw the attention of Council members to
document S/2016/1126, which contains the text ofa letter
dated 29 December 2016 from the Secretary-General
addressed to the President of the Security Council.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.

A vote was taken by show ofhands.

In favour:
