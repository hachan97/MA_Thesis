The President: As this is the first public meeting
of the Security Council for the month of September, I
should like to take this opportunity, on behalf of the
Security Council, to pay tribute to His Excellency
Ambassador Amr Aboulatta, Permanent Representative
of Egypt, for his service as President of the Council for
the month of August. I am sure I speak for all members
of the Security Council in expressing deep appreciation
to Ambassador Aboulatta and his delegation for the
great diplomatic skill with which they conducted the
Council's business last month.
