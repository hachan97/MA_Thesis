The President: I should like to take this
opportunity to pay tribute, on behalf of the Council, to
His Excellency Mr. Gustavo Meza-Cuadra, Permanent
Representative of Peru, for his service in the presidency
of the Security Council for the month of July. I am sure
I speak for all the members of the Council in expressing
our deep appreciation to Ambassador Meza-Cuadra
and his team for the great diplomatic skill with which
they conducted the Council's business last month.

Expression of welcome to the new Permanent
Representative of China

The President: I should also like to extend, on behalf
of the Council, a warm welcome to the new Permanent
Representative of China to the United Nations, His
Excellency Mr. Zhang Jun. We look forward to working
