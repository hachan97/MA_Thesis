The President (spake in Spanish): In accordance
with the understanding reached in the Council's prior
consultations, I shall take it that the Security Council
agrees to extend invitations under rule 39 of its
provisional rules of procedure to Mr. Thabo Mbeki,
Chairperson of the African Union High-Level Panel on
Darfur; Mr. Djibril Bassole, Joint African Union-
United Nations Chief Mediator for Darfur; Mr. Ibrahim
Gambari, Joint African Union-United Nations Special
Representative for Darfur; and Mr. Haile Menkerios,
Special Representative of the Secretary-General for the
Sudan.

There being no objection, it is so decided.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda. The Council is
meeting in accordance with the understanding reached
in its prior consultations.

At this meeting, the Security Council will hear
briefings by Mr. Mbeki, Mr. Bassole', Mr. Gambari and
Mr. Menkerios. I now give the floor to Mr. Mbeki.
