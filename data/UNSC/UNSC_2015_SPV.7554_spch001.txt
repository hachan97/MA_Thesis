The President: In accordance with rule 37 of the
Council's provisional rules of procedure, I invite the
representative of Somalia to participate in this meeting.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them
document S/2015/850, which contains the text ofa draft
resolution submitted by France, Lithuania, Malaysia,
New Zealand, Spain, the United Kingdom of Great
Britain and Northern Ireland and the United States of
America.

I wish to draw the attention of Council members
to document S/2015/776, which contains the report of
the Secretary-General on the situation with respect
to piracy and armed robbery at sea off the coast of
Somalia.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.
