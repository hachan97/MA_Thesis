The President (spoke in Russian): The Security
Council will now begin its consideration of the item on
its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them document
S/2015/7ll, which contains the text of a draft resolution
submitted by France, Malaysia, the United Kingdon
of Great Britain and Northern Ireland and the United
States of America.

I wish to draw the attention of Council members
to document S/2015/620, which contains the thirtieth
progress report of the Secretary-General on the United
Nations Mission in Liberia.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.
