The President (spoke in Arabic): In accordance
with rule 37 of the Council's provisional rules of
procedure, I invite the representative of Haiti to
participate in this meeting.

The Security Council will now begin its consideration
of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them document
S/2019/Sl9, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by the United States of America.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
Belgium, Cote d'Ivoire, Equatorial Guinea, France,
Germany, Indonesia, Kuwait, Peru, Poland, Russian
Federation, South Africa, United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States
of America

Abstaining:
China, Dominican Republic

The President (Spoke in Arabic): The draft
resolution received 13 votes in favour and two
abstentions. The draft resolution has been adopted as
resolution 2476 (2019).

I shall now give the floor to those members of the
Council who wish to make statements.
