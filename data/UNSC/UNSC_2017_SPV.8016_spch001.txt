The President (spoke in Arabic): As this is the
first public meeting of the Security Council for the
month of August, I should like to take this opportunity,
on behalf of the Security Council, to pay tribute to
His Excellency Ambassador Liu Jieyi, Permanent
Representative of China, for his service as President of
the Council for the month of July. I am sure I speak for
all members of the Security Council in expressing deep
appreciation to Ambassador Liu and his delegation for
the great diplomatic skill with which they conducted
the Council's business last month.

Expression of welcome to the new Permanent
Representative of the Russian Federation

The President (spoke in Arabic): I should also like
to extend, on behalf of the Council, a warm welcome
to the new Permanent Representative of the Russian
Federation to the United Nations, His Excellency
Mr. Vassily Alekseevich Nebenzia. We look forward
to collaborating closely with him and we wish him
every success.
