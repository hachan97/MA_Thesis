I have the honour to transmit herewith a copy of the briefing provided by the Permanent Representative of Estonia, Sven Jiirgenson, in his capacity as Chair of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 1591 (2005) concerning the Sudan, in connection with the videoconference on the item entitled "Reports of the Secretary-General on the Sudan and South Sudan", convened on 11 December 2020 (see annex). In accordance with the procedure set out in the letter dated 7 May 2020 from the President of the Security Council addressed to the Permanent Representatives of the members of the Security Council (S/2020/372), which was agreed upon in the light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the coronavirus disease (COVID-19) pandemic, these briefings and statements are to be issued as documents of the Council.
