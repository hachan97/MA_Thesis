The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them document
S/2016/659, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by France.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
France, Japan, Malaysia, New Zealand, Russian
Federation, Senegal, Spain, Ukraine, United
Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland,
United States of America and Uruguay

Abstaining:
Angola, China, Egypt, Venezuela

The President: There were 11 votes in favour, none
against and 4 abstentions. The draft resolution has been
adopted as resolution 2303 (2016).

I shall now give the floor to those members of
the Council who wish to make statements following
the voting.
