The President: Under rule 39 of the Council's
provisional rules of procedure, I invite Mr. Roger
Meece, Special Representative of the Secretary-
General and Head of the United Nations Organization
Stabilization Mission in the Democratic Republic of the
Congo, to participate in this meeting.

On behalf of the Council, I welcome Mr. Meece,
who is joining today's meeting via video teleconference
from Kinshasa.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda. Members have
before them document 8/2012/838, which contains the
report of the Secretary-General on the United Nations
Organization Stabilization Mission in the Democratic
Republic of the Congo.

I now give the floor to Mr. Meece.
