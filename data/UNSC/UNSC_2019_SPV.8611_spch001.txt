opportunity, on behalf of the Council, to pay tribute
to Her Excellency Ms. Joanna Wronecka, Permanent

The President (spoke in Russian): In accordance
with rule 37 of the Council's provisional rules of
procedure, I invite the representative of Libya to

provisional rules of procedure, I invite the following

Libya.

Mr. Salame is joining the meeting via video-
teleconference from Tripoli.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda.

I wish to draw the attention of Council members
to document S/ZOl9/682, which contains the report of
the Secretary-General on the United Nations Support
Mission in Libya.

briefings by Mr. Salame; Ambassador Juergen Schulz,
Deputy Permanent Representative of Germany, in his
capacity as Chair of the Security Council Committee

concerning Libya; and Ms. Mohamed.
