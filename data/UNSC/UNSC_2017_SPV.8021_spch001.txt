The President (spoke in Arabic): The Security
Council will now begin its consideration of the item on
its agenda.

The Security Council will now proceed to the
consideration of its annual report to the General
Assembly, for the period from 1 August 2015 to
31 December 2016.

The draft report has been provided to the members
of the Council. The introduction to the draft report was
prepared by the delegation of Japan, as President of the
Council in July 2016, with the participation of the other
members of the Council. The body of the report was
prepared by the Secretariat. I should like to express my
appreciation to Japan and to the Secretariat for their
respective contributions.

1 should like to point out that the draft report was
prepared in accordance with the notes by the President
of 26 July 2010 (S/2010/507) and of 10 December 2015
(S/2015/944).

I now give the floor to the representative of Japan,
whose delegation was responsible for drafting the
introduction to this year's annual report.
