The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

The Security Council will now proceed to the
consideration of its annual report to the General
Assembly, for the period from 1 January 2017 to
31 December 2017.

The draft report has been provided to the members
of the Council. The introduction to the draft report was
prepared by the delegation of China, as President of the
Council in July 2017, with the participation of the other
members of the Council. The body of the report was
prepared by the Secretariat. I should like to express my
appreciation to China and to the Secretariat for their
respective contributions.

I should like to point out that the draft report was
prepared in accordance with the note by the President
of 30 August 2017 (S/2017/507).

I now give the floor to the representative of China,
whose delegation was responsible for drafting the
introduction to this year's annual report.
