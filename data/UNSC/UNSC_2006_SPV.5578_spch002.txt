Mr. Kalomoh (Assistant Secretary-General for Political Affairs): I have the great pleasure to present
the draft annual report of the Security Council for the
2

period 1 August 2005 to 31 July 2006, to be submitted
to the General Assembly pursuant to Article 24,
paragraph 3, and Article 15, paragraph 1, of the Charter
of the United Nations. The factual information
contained in the draft report before the Council was
prepared by the Secretariat in accordance with the
provisions of the note by the President of the Security
Council of 22 May 2002, contained in document
S/2002/199, and now incorporated in the note by the
President of 19 July 2006, contained in document
S/2006/507.

The draft report before the Council provides a
guide to the activities of the Council in a concise and
comprehensive manner. Of particular interest is the
introduction to the report, which was prepared by the
delegation of France. The Secretariat wishes to express
its appreciation for the introduction by the delegation
of France.
