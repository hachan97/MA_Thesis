The President (spoke in Spanish): The Security
Council will now begin its consideration of the item on
its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them document
8/2018/970, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by the United States of America.

I wish to draw the attention of Council members
to document S/2018/889, which contains the report
of the Secretary-General on the situation concerning
Western Sahara.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
China, C6te d'Ivoire, Equatorial Guinea, France,
Kazakhstan, Kuwait, Netherlands, Peru, Poland,
Sweden, United Kingdom of Great Britain and
Northern Ireland, United States of America

Against:
None

Abstaining:
Bolivia (Plurinational State of), Ethiopia, Russian
Federation

The President (spoke in Spanish): The draft
resolution received 12 votes in favour, none against
and three abstentions. The draft resolution has been
adopted as resolution 2440 (2018).

I now give the floor to those members of the
Council who wish to make statements after the voting.
