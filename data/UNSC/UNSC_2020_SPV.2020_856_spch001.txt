I have the honour to refer to Security Council resolution 2538 (2020), in connection with the item entitled "United Nations peacekeeping operations" and adopted on 28 August 2020. Resolution 2538 (2020) was adopted in accordance with the voting procedure set out in the letter by the President of the Security Council dated 27 March 2020 (S/2020/253), a procedure agreed in the light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the coronavirus disease pandemic. Pursuant to that procedure, I enclose herewith copies of the related documents: My letter dated 27 August 2020, addressed to the Permanent Representatives of the members of the Security Council (see annex I), putting to the vote the draft resolution contained in document S/2020/838 (see enclosure to annex I); Letters received in reply from members of the Security Council indicating their national positions on the draft resolution (see annexes II to XVI); Statements submitted subsequently by Security Council members providing their explanations of vote (see annexes XVII and XVIII). This letter and its annexes will be issued as a document of the Security Council.
