The President: In accordance with rule 37 of
the Council's provisional rules of procedure, I invite
the representative of South Sudan to participate in
this meeting.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them document
S/2016/705, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by the United States of America.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
Angola, France, Japan, Malaysia, New Zealand,
Senegal, Spain, Ukraine, United Kingdom of Great
Britain and Northern Ireland, United States of
America and Uruguay

Abstaining:
China, Egypt, Russian Federation and Venezuela
(Bolivarian Republic of)

The President: The draft resolution received 11
votes in favour, none against and four abstentions. The
draft resolution has been adopted as resolution 2304
(2016).

I shall now give the floor to those members of
the Council who wish to make statements following
the voting.
