Peacekeeping Operations; Mr. Issoufou Yacouba, Police

Integrated Stabilization Mission in Mali; Mr. Georges-

8/2017/926, which contains the text ofa draft resolution

draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution

Force Commanders. We look forward to questions from
Council members to the Commissioners and from the
Commissioners to the Council.
