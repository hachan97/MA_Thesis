The President: I should like to take this
opportunity, on behalf of the Security Council, to pay
tribute to His Excellency Ambassador Gustavo Meza-
Cuadra, Permanent Representative of Peru to the United
Nations, for his service as President of the Council for
the month of April. I am sure I speak for all members
of the Security Council in expressing deep appreciation
to Ambassador Meza-Cuadra and his team for the
great diplomatic skill with which they conducted the
Council's business last month.
