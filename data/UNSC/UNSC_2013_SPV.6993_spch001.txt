The President: As this is the first meeting of the
Council for the month of July 2013, I should like to take
this opportunity to pay tribute, on behalf of the Council,
to His Excellency Ambassador Sir Mark Lyall Grant,
Permanent Representative of the United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland, for his service as
President of the Security Council for the month of June
2013. I am sure I speak for all members of the Council
in expressing deep appreciation to Ambassador Lyall
Grant and his team for the great diplomatic skill with
which they conducted the Council's business last month.
