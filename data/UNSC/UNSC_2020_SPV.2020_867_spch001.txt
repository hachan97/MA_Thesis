I have the honour to refer to Security Council resolution 2541 (2020), in connection with the agenda item "The situation in Mali". The resolution was adopted on 31 August 2020 in accordance with the voting procedure set out in the letter from the President of the Security Council dated 27 March 2020 (S/2020/253), a procedure agreed in the light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the coronavirus disease pandemic Pursuant to that procedure, I enclose herewith copies of the related documents: My letter dated 28 August 2020, addressed to the Permanent Representatives of the members of the Security Council (see annex I), putting to the vote the draft resolution contained in document S/2020/850 (see enclosure to annex I); Letters received in reply from members of the Security Council indicating their national position on the draft resolution (annexes II to XVI); Statements subsequently submitted by members of the Security Council providing explanations of their votes (annexes XVII and XVIII). The present letter and its annexes will be issued as an official document of the Security Council.
