The President: As this is the first public meeting
of the Council for the month of September, I should like
to take this opportunity to pay tribute, on behalf of the
Council, to His Excellency Ambassador Ramlan Bin
Ibrahim, Permanent Representative of Malaysia, for
his service as President of the Council for the month of
August. I am sure I speak for all members ofthe Council
in expressing deep appreciation to Ambassador Ibrahim
and his delegation for the great diplomatic skill with
which they conducted the Council's business last month.
