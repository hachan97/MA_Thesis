The President (spoke in Chinese): In view of
the request and comments made by members of the
Security Council, I intend to put the provisional agenda
to the vote.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
provisional agenda for today's meeting. I shall put the
provisional agenda to the vote now.

A vote was taken by show ofhana's.

In favour:
Bolivia (Plurinational State of), China, Kazakhstan,
Russian Federation

Against:
France, Kuwait, Netherlands, Poland, Sweden,
United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern
Ireland, United States of America

Abstaining:
Cote d'Ivoire, Equatorial Guinea, Ethiopia, Peru

The President (spoke in Chinese): The provisional
agenda received 4 votes in favour, 7 votes against and
4 abstentions. The provisional agenda has not been
adopted, having failed to obtain the required number
of votes.

I now give the floor to those members of the
Council who wish to make statements after the voting.
