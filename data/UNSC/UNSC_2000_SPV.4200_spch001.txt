The President (spoke in French): In accordance
with the understanding reached in the Council's prior
consultations, and in the absence of objection, I shall
take it that the Council agrees to extend an invitation
under rule 39 of its provisional rules of procedure to
Mr. Bernard Kouchner, Special Representative of the
Secretary-General and Head of the United Nations
Interim Administration Mission in Kosovo, Federal
Republic of Yugoslavia.

There being no objection, it is so decided.

I welcome Mr. Kouchner and invite him to take a
seat at the Council table.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda. The Security
Council is meeting in accordance with the
understanding reached in its prior consultations.

Members of the Council have before them the
report of the Secretary-General on the United Nations
Interim Administration Mission in Kosovo, document
S/2000/878.

At this meeting, the Security Council will hear a
briefing by Mr. Bernard Kouchner, Special
Representative of the Secretary-General and Head of
the United Nations Interim Administration Mission in
Kosovo, Federal Republic of Yugoslavia. I give him the
floor.
