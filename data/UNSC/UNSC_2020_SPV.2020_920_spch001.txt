I have the honour to refer to Security Council resolution 2544 (2020), related to the agenda item "Threats to international peace and security" and adopted on 18 September 2020. Resolution 2544 (2020) was adopted in accordance with the voting procedure set out in the letter by the President of the Security Council dated 27 March 2020 (S/2020/253), a procedure agreed in the light of the extraordinary circumstances caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. Pursuant to that procedure, I enclose herewith copies of the related documents: My letter dated 17 September 2020, addressed to the Permanent Representatives of Security Council members, putting to the vote the draft resolution contained in document S/2020/910 (see annex I and enclosure); Letters received in reply from Security Council members indicating their national positions on the draft resolution (see annexes II to XVI). This letter and its annexes will be issued as a document of the Security Council.
