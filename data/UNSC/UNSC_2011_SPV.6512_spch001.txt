The President (spoke in Spanish): Under rule 37
of the Council's provisional rules of procedure, I invite
the representatives of Italy, Somalia, Spain and
Ukraine to participate in this meeting.

The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them
document S/2011/228, which contains the text of a
draft resolution submitted by Colombia, Denmark,
France, Gabon, India, Italy, Lebanon, Nigeria,
Portugal, the Russian Federation, Spain and Ukraine.

It is my understanding that the Council is ready
to proceed to the vote on the draft resolution before it.

I shall now put the draft resolution to the vote.
A vote was taken by show of hands.

In favour:
Bosnia and Herzegovina, Brazil, China,
Colombia, France, Gabon, Germany, India,
Lebanon, Nigeria, Portugal, Russian Federation,
South Africa, United Kingdom of Great Britain
and Northern Ireland, United States of America.

The President (spoke in Spanish): There were 15
votes in favour. The draft resolution has been adopted
unanimously as resolution 1976 (2011).

I now give the floor to the representative of the
Russian Federation, who wishes to speak following the
adoption of the resolution.
