procedure, I invite the representatives of Afghanistan,
Australia, Canada, Egypt, India, the Islamic Republic

Uzbekistan to participate in this meeting.

provisional rules of procedure, I invite the following

General and Head of the United Nations Assistance

Executive Director of the United Nations Office on

teleconference from Vienna.

provisional rules of procedure, I invite His Excellency

consideration of the item on its agenda.

Secretary-General on the situation in Afghanistan and

Representative of Indonesia, in his capacity as Chair of
the Security Council Committee established pursuant
to resolution 1988 (2011).
