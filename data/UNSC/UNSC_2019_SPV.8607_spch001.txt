The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them document
S/2019/687, which contains the text ofa draft resolution
submitted by the France.

I wish to draw the attention of Council members
to document S/2019/636, which contains the text of a
letter dated 6 August 2019 from the Panel of Experts
established pursuant to resolution 2374 (2017) on Mali
addressed to the President of the Security Council.

The Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the
draft resolution before it. I shall put the draft resolution
to the vote now.

A vote was taken by Show ofhands.

In favour:
Belgium, China, Cete d'lvoire, Dominican
Republic, Equatorial Guinea, France, Germany,
Indonesia, Kuwait, Peru, Poland, Russian
Federation, South Africa, United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States
of America

The President: The draft resolution received 15
votes in favour. The draft resolution has been adopted
unanimously as resolution 2484 (2019).
