The President: The Council is ready to proceed to
the vote on the draft resolution before it. I shall put the
draft resolution to the vote now.

A vote was taken by show ofhands.
In favour:
Angola, Chad, Chile, China, France, Jordan,
Lithuania, Malaysia, New Zealand, Nigeria, Spain,

(Bolivarian Republic of)

Abstaining:

The President: There were 14 votes in favour, none
against and l abstention. The draft resolution has been
adopted as resolution 2256 (2015).
