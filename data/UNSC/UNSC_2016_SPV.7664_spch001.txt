The President (spoke in Chinese): As this is the
first formal meeting of the Security Council for the
month of April, I should like to take this opportunity to
pay tribute, on behalf of the Council, to His Excellency
Mr. Ismael Gaspar Martins, Permanent Representative
of Angola, for his service as President of the Council for
the month of March 2016. I am sure I speak for all the
members of the Council in expressing deep appreciation
to Ambassador Gaspar Martins and his delegation for
the great diplomatic skill with which they conducted
the Council's business last month.
