The President: The Security Council will now
proceed to the consideration of its annual report to
the General Assembly, for the period from 1 August
2012 to 31 July 2013.

The draft report has been provided to members
of the Council. The introduction to the draft report
was prepared by the delegation of the United States
of America, as President of the Council in July 2013,
with the participation of the other members of the
Council. The body of the report was prepared by the
Secretariat. 1 should like to express appreciation to the
United States of America and the Secretariat for their
respective contributions.

1 should like to point out that the draft report was
prepared in accordance with the note by the President
of 26 July 2010 (S/2010/507).

I now give the floor to the representative of the
United States, whose delegation was responsible for
drafting the introduction to this year's annual report.
