The President: At the outset of the meeting, I
should like, on behalf of the members of the Security
Council, to ask that all those present now stand and
join in observing a minute of silence in memory of the
victims of the heinous terrorist act perpetrated at
Domodedovo International Airport in Moscow on
24 January 2011. We express sincere sympathy and
condolences to the relatives of the citizens of the
Russian Federation and other countries who were
killed or injured.

The members of the Security Council observed a
minute of silence.

Adoption of the agenda
The agenda was adopted.
