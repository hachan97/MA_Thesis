The President (spoke in Russian): The Security
Council will now begin its consideration of the item on
its agenda.

Members of the Council have before them documents
S/2014/409 and S/2014/408, each of which contains the
text of a draft resolution. It is my understanding that
the Council is ready to proceed to the vote on the draft
resolutions before it.

I shall first put to the vote the draft resolution
contained in document S/2014/409, submitted by
Australia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, the United Kingdom
of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, the United States
of America and France.

A vote was taken by a Show ofhands.

In favour:
Argentina, Australia, Chad, Chile, China, France,
Jordan, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Nigeria, Republic
of Korea, Russian Federation, Rwanda, United
Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
and United States of America

The President (spoke in Russian): There were
15 votes in favour. The draft resolution has been
adopted unanimously as resolution 2160 (2014).

I shall now put to the vote the draft resolution
contained in document S/2014/408, submitted by
Australia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, the United Kingdom
of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, the United States
of America and France.

A vote was taken by a Show ofhands.

In favour:
Argentina, Australia, Chad, Chile, China, France,
Jordan, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Nigeria, Republic
of Korea, Russian Federation, Rwanda, United
Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
and United States of America


The President (spoke in Russian): There were
15 votes in favour. The draft resolution has been
adopted unanimously as resolution 2161 (2014).

The Security Council has thus concluded the present
stage of its consideration of the item on its agenda.

The meeting rose at 10.10 am.
