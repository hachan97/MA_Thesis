The President: The Council is ready to proceed to
the vote on the draft resolution before it. I shall put the
draft resolution to the vote now.

A vote was taken by show ofhands.

In favour:
Bolivia (Plurinational State of), China, Kazakhstan,
Russian Federation

Against:
France, Italy, Sweden, Ukraine, United Kingdom of
Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States
of America, Uruguay

Abstaining:
Egypt, Ethiopia, Japan, Senegal

The President: The draft resolution received
4 votes in favour, 7 against and 4 abstentions. The draft
resolution is not adopted, having failed to obtain the
required number of votes.

I shall now give the floor those members who wish
to make statements after the voting.
