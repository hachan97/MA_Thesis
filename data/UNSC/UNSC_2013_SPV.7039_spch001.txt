The President: As this is the first meeting of the
Council for the month of October, I should like to take
this opportunity to pay tribute, on behalf of the Council,
to His Excellency Mr. Gary Quinlan, Permanent
Representative of Australia, for his service as President
of the Council for the month of September. I am sure I
speak for all members of the Council in expressing deep
appreciation to Ambassador Quinlan and his delegation
for the great diplomatic skill with which they conducted
the Council's business last month.

Adoption of the agenda
The agenda was adopted.
The situation in the Middle East

The President: The Security Council will now
begin its consideration of the item on its agenda.

The Council has before it the text of a statement by
the President on behalf of the Council on the subject
of today's meeting. I thank Council members for their
valuable contributions to this statement. In accordance
with the understanding reached among the members
of the Council, I shall take it that the members of the
Security Council agree to the statement, which will be
issued as a document of the Security Council under the
symbol S/PRST/2013/15.

It is so decided.

The Security Council has thus concluded the present
stage of its consideration of the item on its agenda.

The meeting rose at 10.55 am.
